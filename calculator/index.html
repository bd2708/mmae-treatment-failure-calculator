About This Calculator

Estimates 90-day treatment failure risk after MMAE for subdural hematoma.

Treatment failure: surgical intervention, repeat MMAE, or SDH-related death within 90 days.

Gradient-boosting model, AUC 0.856. Displayed probabilities are Platt-calibrated to the published model; inference runs entirely in your browser.

Intended for overall risk stratification. The effect of a single input should not be interpreted in isolation.

When to Use
  • Pre-procedural risk stratification
  • Shared decision-making
  • Post-procedural surveillance planning

Not a substitute for clinical judgment.
